What is fraud?
  • Candidates from each party can 'commit a fraud'
  • If a candidate from a party decides to commit a fraud, then one vote for his/her party will be added, and one vote for the opposite party will be taken away.
    • {% if subsession.info %}
  • Candidates can observe decisions regarding fraud of each other
  • They can inform voters whether a candidate of another is planning to commit a fraud.
  • It may or may not correspond to the real decision of another candidate.
    • {% endif %}
